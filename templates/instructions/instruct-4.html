B) Part one: The interactive ACME search engine exercise (continued)

This is not a memory test. However, when answering these questions, it is important that during each trail you take into account the number of relevant ‘green’ links received and the nature of the advertisement displayed i.e. does the advertisement appear to directly link to the current or previous search terms used? Please take a few seconds to think about all this information before submitting your responses at the end of each trial. You can always check the to-do list if unsure about the search terms that appeared. The answers you provide will be used to group you together with other participants that have similar answers. From this information, we will calculate whether you will receive a bonus of $1.5 (US dollars). Note that your answers will be reviewed to determine if they meet a minimum level of consistency, indicating thoughtful and honest responses. You will have the chance to win an extra bonus if your answers during the last (12th) interaction with the ACME search engine is the closest one to the mean of the answers in your group.
