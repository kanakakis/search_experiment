STUDY INSTRUCTIONS: ONLINE EXERCISE (1/7)

A) The design of this online study
This online study is split into two parts: 1) an ACME Search Engine exercise; and, 2) a questionnaire.

Please set aside at least 40 minutes of your time to complete this study.
You must complete and submit your entire responses to this study in one continuous session.
You will not be able to save your progress and return to this online study at a later time. Whilst the estimated time for completing this study is around 40 minutes, if not completed within 120 minutes your session will time-out. If you are unable to complete the study within the allotted time, you can start a new session from the beginning, but none of your progress from the previous session will be saved. You will only be able to complete this study once.

B) Part one: The interactive ACME search engine exercise
The scenario:
You are planning a surprise birthday party and need to find a set of ten (10) items, such as a bespoke birthday cake. You will use the fictional ACME search engine to attempt to locate these items. Additionally, you will use the ACME search engine to perform a couple of more queries for a side-project. Thus this exercise consists of twelve (12) trials, one for each action item in the following to-do list.



The search keywords
The keywords that you input into the ACME search engine are already pre-defined in accordance with each scenario. Therefore, all you have to do is press the search button. You cannot input any of your own keywords into the search bar.

The search results
A list of search results (delivered by the ACME search engine) will appear when you press the search button. These search results will appear in a list form. These search results will not look like normal search results i.e. there are no links to websites or descriptions provided.
